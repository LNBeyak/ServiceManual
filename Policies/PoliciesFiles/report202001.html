Report for Monday, January 20, 2020

Book Club: "Everybody Lies" by Seth Stephens-Davidowitz
Welcome Table

At the Welcome table, participants registered by completing the member survey.

They made nametags for themselves.

They were given a copy of the last month's banking statement and an account balance report, reconciled.

They were given an Update Exercise worksheet and a handout about becoming a member.

Updates

Anna Kirkland Smith, president, led each participant in relating her Update Exercise.

Discussion

Julia Kuznetsova, Director of Programs, provided a handout of book club questions.

The book's author, Seth Stephens-Davidowitz, has numerous publications, including the peer-reviewed paper Association between an Internet-Based Measure of Area Racism and Black Mortality published by the Public Library of Science.

His personal website (sethsd.com) offers several data sets.

Group Announcements

Anna Kirkland Smith, president invited everyone to become a founding member.
